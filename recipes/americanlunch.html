American School Lunch

This meal stokes joy into the heart of children around the nation! You can serve it hot or cold, they don't have much of a choice!

Ingredients

  • 9 old baby carrots. You want these really dry.
  • 1 carton of fat free chocolate milk.
  • 2 packets of the most off-brand ketchup you can find.
  • 1 small cup of bbq dipping sauce to add flavor and variety to an already amazing meal.
  • 1/2 cup of cooked white rice.
  • 3 1/2 turd shaped chicken? strips

Steps

  1. Put each item into a seperate area ordained by the almighty tray. Really makes it look like more than it is.
  2. Be sure to not salt or pepper the rice!
  3. Charge the FUCK out that kid.
